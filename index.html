Hi there! I'm Fabio.

I'm a Software Engineer based in Stockholm. I'm head of engineering at Hopsworks AB the company developing the Hopsworks feature store.

Hopsworks is a Feature Store providing data scientists with a unique platform to govern, share and make features available for training models, batch scoring and building real time prediction services. Hopsworks sits in between the modern data stack (e.g. Snowflake, Data Lakes, Streams) and the data scientists. It provides them with clear and python centric APIs to tap into the vast amount of existing enterprise data and allows them to build and productionize models with the tools they are most familiar with (e.g. Python, Pandas, PyTorch). If you are curious, you can start building on Hopsworks today at https://app.hopsworks.ai

In my current role I'm responsible for the design and implementation of the feature store component. I spend a significant amount of my time interacting with customers and users to understand their needs and requirements, and then coordinate the implementation.
