Hi there! I'm Fabio.

I'm an Italian Software Engineer based in Stockholm. I'm head of engineering at Logical Clocks AB the company leading the development efforts of the HopsHadoop platform.

Hops is a big data platform with a focus on Deep Learning. The platform makes it easy for Data Scientists to achieve hyperscale training for their DL models with tools such as TensorFlowOnSpark and Horovod. It provides GPUs-as-a-resource, Jupyter and PySpark, management of Conda environments and a lot of other goodies. It is based on HopsFS, the world's most scalable file system. A fork of Apache HDFS, which stores files' and directories' metadata on a distributed, new sql database. We wrote a bunch of peer reviewed papers on the platform, have a look!

Currently, I'm focusing my efforts on building a scalable model serving infrastructure, based on Kubernetes and integrated with the Hops platform and its security model based on X.509 certificates. I'm also involved in the design and development of a Feature Store for Hops and integrated with TensorFlow. Previously I modified the Apache Hive metastore to achieve strong consistency between the metadata on the database and the files on HopsFS. You can read my Master Thesis for more details.
